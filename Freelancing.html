Freelancing - Take Your Skills To The Market

In the current difficult situation with self isolation more and more people are
leaving offices for remote work. This short course will help you take your skills
to the market. We will train you to work on various freelance marketplaces like
Fiverr, Upwork, Guru, and other channels. This course is perfect for those who
are already skilled in their respective niche and want to grow their career.
