AboutE- Commerce

Demand for specialists in the field of e-commerce is constantly growing. The e-commerce market is growing annually. More than 70% if all regular internet users are clients of e-commerce. In this 3 months course of e-commerce you will be able to work as an e-commerce developer for existing enterprises, an e-commerce consultant or adviser in the international market.

What will you learn?
The concept of e-commerce development.
ERP system and e-commerce.
E-business management.
Electronic Payment systems.
Interner project management.
Marketing communication on internet.
Evaluation of e-commerce efficiency.
Electronic Logistics.
